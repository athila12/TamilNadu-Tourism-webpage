About Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu, the southern gateway to India, is a land of timeless temples, classical arts, lush hills, historic towns, and flavorful cuisine. Explore:
  • Ancient Civilizations: Sangam period legacy, classical Tamil literature, and rich tradition.
  • Architectural Marvels: Temples like Meenakshi Amman, Brihadeeswara & Ramanathaswamy, renowned for their gopurams/festivals.
  • Cultural Arts: Birthplace of Bharatanatyam dance, Carnatic music, Tanjore painting, and bronze sculpture.
  • Festivals & Rituals: Pongal, Chithirai, Natyanjali, Jallikattu, and hundreds of celebrations.
  • Hill Stations & Beaches: Ooty, Kodaikanal, Marina Beach, Valparai, Yercaud and charming hill railways.
  • Signature Cuisine: Chettinad curries, dosai, filter coffee, Kanchipuram silk sarees, and crafts.
  • UNESCO Heritage: Mahabalipuram, Living Chola Temples, Nilgiri Mountain Railways.
Experience a state where the ancient and modern intertwine.
